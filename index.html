Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to pay anything to see my preview? +
Absolutely not. We build your full website completely free — no card, no deposit, zero upfront cost. You only pay if you see it, love it, and want to go live. If you don't like it, you walk away and pay nothing. That's the whole model.
What does "unlimited amendments" actually mean? +
Exactly what it says. For 12 months from when your site goes live, you can request any change — new services, updated prices, different photos, new sections, tweaked copy — as many times as you like. We make every change free of charge, no questions asked.
What's included with the free domain and email? +
We register a .co.uk domain in your business name (e.g. devansfade.co.uk), connect it to your website, and set it all up — included in your package at no extra cost. We also set up a professional business email address (e.g. hello@devansfade.co.uk) so you look the part from day one. Both are fully handled by us.
Are there any monthly fees after I pay? +
No monthly fees from us, ever. You pay once and you own the website outright. The only ongoing cost is your domain renewal — typically £10–15 per year — which you pay directly to the domain registrar.
What if I want changes before I pay? +
That's the whole point of the preview. Your site is a draft — change anything you want before committing. Different layout, different colours, different photos, different text — just tell us and we'll update it for free until it's exactly right. You pay nothing until you're 100% happy.
How does the referral commission work? +
If you refer another business to us and they go live with a WebCraft UK website, we send you 30% of whatever they pay — that's £150 for a Starter site, up to £600+ for Premium. Just make sure they mention your name when they get in touch and we'll handle the rest. There's no limit on how many referrals you make.
How long does it take to go live? +
Once you confirm you're happy and make payment, your site is live and connected to your domain within 48 hours. Fast, simple, sorted.
What types of businesses do you work with? +
All types of UK small businesses — barbers, hair and beauty salons, personal trainers, restaurants, cafés, plumbers, electricians, builders, nail bars, tattoo studios, landscapers, cleaners, and much more. If you run a business in the UK, we can build your site.
